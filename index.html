Dataverse for Teams — Reduced 13-Table Model

The application behavior is unchanged. Related setup data is consolidated to reduce maintenance while preserving Division/Section routing, approvals, completions, reporting, notifications, and audit history.

#Dataverse TablePurposeConsolidation
1IDP Organization UnitDivision and Section hierarchy, supervisor, and default approval group.Replaces separate Division and Section tables.
2IDP EmployeeMicrosoft 365 identity, organization assignment, and app role.Role is a Choice on Employee.
3IDP Program YearAnnual dates, default target, escalation threshold, and default times.Includes former system settings.
4IDP Employee TargetEmployee-specific target by Program Year.Preserves historical annual targets.
5IDP Reference ValuePlatform, Class Type, and Category values.Replaces three reference tables.
6IDP Approval GroupSection/Division scope and approval method.Unchanged business function.
7IDP Approval Group MemberApprover membership, order, and completion-review permission.Unchanged business function.
8IDP Training SessionOptional scheduled offerings and capacity.Supports reusable sessions.
9IDP Training RequestEmployee request, planned values, routing snapshots, status, and correction tracking.Core transaction.
10IDP ApprovalSection, Division, and Completion decisions with required reasons.One decision-history table.
11IDP Training CompletionActual date/time/hours, variance, certificate, and review status.Includes former Certificate table.
12IDP NotificationIn-app/email notification queue and delivery state.Unchanged business function.
13IDP Audit LogActor, timestamp, reason, and previous/new values.Custom audit retained.
StepPhaseActorActionDecision / ControlNext Status / Route
1PlanningEmployeeIdentify a training need that supports the Individual Development Plan.Training must have a valid business purpose.Open New Training Request.
2RequestEmployeeEnter training title, platform, class type, category, date, start/end time, requested hours, cost, and justification.Required fields must be complete.Draft request is ready for validation.
3RequestPower AppsValidate active Program Year, required fields, 30-minute times, End > Start, and Requested Hours ≤ selected time window.Invalid data is blocked with a field message.Valid request continues.
4RequestApp / Data SourceGenerate Request ID, timestamp, organization snapshot, and audit record; save the request.Request ID must be unique.Status = Pending Section Approval.
5Section ApprovalAutomationResolve the active Section Approval Group, create eligible reviewer records, and notify members.A configured active group is required.Route to Section Approval Queue.
6Section ApprovalSection ApproverReview the request, justification, planned date/time, hours, cost, and employee scope.Membership, priority, and Any One / All / Sequential method are enforced.Approve, Return, or Reject.
7ACorrectionSection ApproverReturn the request to the employee.A return reason is required.Status = Returned; employee notified.
7BCorrectionEmployeeEdit the returned record and resubmit it.Owner only; all validation repeats; before/after values are audited.Status = Pending; return to Step 5.
7CClosureSection ApproverReject the request.A rejection reason is required.Status = Rejected; employee notified.
7DClosureEmployeeCancel an eligible Pending, Pending Division Approval, or Returned request.A cancellation reason is required.Status = Cancelled; audit and notification recorded.
8Section ApprovalApproval GroupComplete the Section approval stage according to the configured method.Required Section approvals must be complete.Evaluate Division escalation.
9Division CheckSystemDetermine whether Division escalation is required.HTML rule: estimated cost ≥ $1,000 and an active Division group exists.Yes → Step 10; No → Step 13.
10Division ApprovalAutomationCreate the Division approval stage and notify eligible Division approvers.Active Division group and eligible members are required.Status = Pending Division Approval.
11Division ApprovalDivision ApproverReview the Section decision and the escalated request.Membership and approval method are enforced.Approve, Return, or Reject.
12ACorrectionDivision ApproverReturn the request with a reason.Employee must correct and resubmit through Section review again.Status = Returned; return to Step 2/5.
12BClosureDivision ApproverReject the request with a reason.Reason and decision audit are required.Status = Rejected; employee notified.
12CDivision ApprovalDivision ApproverApprove the escalation.Configured approval method must be complete.Continue to final approval.
13ApprovedSystemSet the request to Approved and notify the employee and approval group.All required approval stages are complete.Employee attends training.
14TrainingEmployeeAttend and complete the approved training.Only an Approved request can be completed.Open Complete Training.
15CompletionEmployeeSelect the Approved Request.Employee owns the request and status is Approved.Load the approved comparison baseline.
16CompletionPower AppsDefault actual date, start time, end time, and hours from the approved request.Employee may change actual values.Display Approved Request vs Actual Completion.
17CompletionEmployeeEnter actual completion date, actual start/end time, actual hours, certificate, and notes.Actual times use 30-minute intervals.Run comparison.
18ComparisonPower AppsCompare approved and actual date, start time, end time, and hours.Every difference is listed; Hours Variance = Actual − Requested.Match confirmed or Variance found.
19VarianceEmployeeProvide a variance explanation when any actual value differs.Explanation is mandatory when Has Variance = Yes.Ready for completion submission.
20CompletionApp / AutomationRevalidate actual time/hours, save completion and variance fields, write audit history, and notify reviewers.Actual End > Start; Actual Hours ≤ actual time window; variance explanation present.Status = Completion Submitted.
21Completion ReviewAutomationRoute the submission to eligible completion reviewers in the same Section Approval Group.Can Review Completion permission is required.Reviewer notified.
22Completion ReviewCompletion ReviewerView approved and actual values side by side, the variance summary, explanation, and evidence.Reviewer eligibility is rechecked.Approve, Return, or Reject.
23ACompletion CorrectionCompletion ReviewerReturn or reject the completion with a reason.Reason is required; parent request returns to Approved.Employee corrects and resubmits from Step 17.
23BCompletedCompletion ReviewerApprove completion and actual hours.Only approved Actual Hours count toward compliance.Request Status = Completed.
24ReportingSystemUpdate Employee Dashboard, Recent Requests, compliance, Division, and Executive reporting.Use approved actual completion data.Reports refreshed.
25Audit & RolloverAdministrator / SystemRetain request, decision, completion, variance, notification, and correction history; perform approved annual rollover.Historical records are retained and not deleted.Lifecycle complete.
